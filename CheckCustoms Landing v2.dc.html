The Problem
"Nobody tells you the real number until your container is already sitting at the terminal, and the clock is running on demurrage."

Clearing charges in Nigeria are built from a mix of NCS duty schedules, VAT, levies, terminal handling and agency fees that shift by HS code, port and season. Most importers only discover the true total after goods have already landed — by then, storage and demurrage are compounding daily.
