How It Works

Two moves. Full visibility.

From the moment you check a cost to the moment your goods are delivered, you see every step — who's responsible, what's next, and how long it should take.

01

Know the cost.

Enter what you're importing — a vehicle by make, model, year and VIN, or a container/goods shipment by category and HS code. We match it against the current NCS tariff and valuation rules engine and return an itemised breakdown: duty, levies, VAT, and port/terminal charges.

Every estimate carries the effective FX rate and rules-engine version that produced it — so you know exactly what it's based on, and when it was last updated.

02

Get it cleared.

Your estimate becomes a clearing request: upload your Bill of Lading, invoice, and Form M/PAAR reference. CheckCustoms ops reviews it and confirms a firm quote.

From there, either CheckCustoms handles clearing directly, or we assign a vetted licensed agent. Either way, CheckCustoms remains your counterparty — you're never dealing with the agent alone.
